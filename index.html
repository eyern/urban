FAQs

What is a vehicle wrap?

A vehicle wrap is a large vinyl graphic or decal that is applied to a vehicle's exterior to change its appearance.

What are the benefits of wrapping a vehicle?

Vehicle wraps offer a cost-effective way to customize a vehicle's appearance, protect the original paint, and can be easily removed

How long do vehicle wraps last?

A high-quality wrap can last anywhere from 5 to 7 years, depending on the material, weather exposure, and maintenance.

Can vehicle wraps damage the paint?

No, when installed and removed correctly, wraps protect the original paint from minor scratches and UV damage.

Are wraps waterproof and car wash safe?

Yes, wraps are waterproof and can be washed in a car wash, but hand washing is recommended for longevity.

How much does a vehicle wrap cost?

Prices vary based on the size of the vehicle, wrap design, and material used, check our pricing section or call us on 0741-913-146.

How long does it take to install a wrap?

Full wraps can take 2-3 days, or even longer for complex vehicles or custom designs
